* **Σ diagonal:** variance compressed along the self-axis (PFC).

$$
\Sigma = 
\begin{bmatrix}
\text{var(self)} & \text{cov(self↔other)} \\
\text{cov(other↔self)} & \text{var(other)}
\end{bmatrix}
$$
